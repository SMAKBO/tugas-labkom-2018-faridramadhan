Physical And Chemical Properties

1. Chemical Identifiers

Dimethyl ether is a colorless gas with a faint ethereal odor. Contact with the liquid can cause frostbite. It is easily ignited. Its vapors are heavier than air. Any leak can be either liquid or vapor. It can asphyxiate by the displacement of air.
